Banana Split

Description

A classic ice cream dessert that is extra popular during the summer months. Banana splits get their name because the a whole banana is split or cut down the middle and that is the first layer of this dessert. Banana splits are typically made with three flavors of ice cream: vanilla, strawberry, and chocolate. They are then topped with hot fudge or chocolate sauce, whipped cream, maraschino cherries and crushed nuts.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1/4 cup vanilla ice cream
  • 1/4 cup strawberry ice cream
  • 1/4 cup chocolate ice cream
  • 2 tablespoons hot fudge sauce
  • 2 tablespoons strawberry sauce
  • 1/2 cup whipped cream
  • 3 maraschino cherries

Steps

  1. Peel the banana and slice in half lengthwise. Place a slice of banana on either side of a medium sized shallow bowl.
  2. Place one scoop each of vanilla, strawberry, and chocolate ice cream, in a row down the center of the bowl. They should be placed in between the banana slices.
  3. Drizzle the hot fudge, caramel sauce, and strawberry over the entire dish. I recommend going side to side from one end of the bananas to the other to get your traditional banana split appearance.
  4. Top each scoop of ice cream with a large dollop of whipped cream or you can pipe it on if you prefer.
  5. Place a maraschino cherry on top of each scoop of whipped cream and sprinkle crushed nuts over the top of the banana split.
  6. Serve immediately.
